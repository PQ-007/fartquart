Turns out I had to write *something* as a first post.

## What kind of site is this?

I learn something, go through something, feel something — and if I feel like it, I write it down, and what I write ends up here. A place to capture whatever I find interesting, in my own way.

---

## What goes in it?

- Highlights from my life
- Contests and events I've taken part in
- Showcases of things I'm building, and series on how I made them
- Interesting algorithms and tech, explained
- Things I got curious about and looked into
- Essays…

---

*This is a kind of archive of the life I've lived, am living, and will live. No comments getting in the way, no pretense, no know-it-alls, no analysis algorithms — just a calm little corner. Posts may be in Mongolian, Japanese, or English, depending on my mood at the time.*
